# Code of Conduct

`rusty-gasket` adopts the [Contributor Covenant 2.1][cc] as its code
of conduct.

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or
invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior
may be reported to the maintainers at
**opensource@godaddy.com**. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

For the full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1, see
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
